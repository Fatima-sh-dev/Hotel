Our Rooms

Unique Stays at Heritage Hostel

We’ve crafted more than just a place to sleep—we offer an experience steeped in tradition, where every room tells a story. Whether you’re a solo adventurer, a couple seeking charm, or a group of friends on a budget, our accommodations blend history, artistry, and comfort for a stay you won’t forget.

The Heritage Dormitory

Step into a vibrant shared space inspired by the communal spirit of our ancestors. Handwoven textiles, reclaimed wood bunk beds, and vintage lanterns set the scene for new friendships. Each bed comes with a personal locker, USB charger, and a bedtime folktale card—because even in a dorm, culture comes first.

The Artisan Private Room

This is where tradition gets cozy. Sleep beneath a ceiling draped in fishing nets (a nod to our coastal heritage) or beside walls adorned with pottery shards turned art. Your private bathroom features a rain shower with hand-painted tiles, and a morning tea set awaits with local blends.

The Storyteller’s Glamping Tent

Five steps from the hostel’s bonfire circle, your luxury canvas tent feels like a chapter from a wanderer’s diary. By day, sunlight filters through embroidered panels; by night, fireflies and folktales come alive. The included breakfast hamper features honey from the hostel’s own hives.
